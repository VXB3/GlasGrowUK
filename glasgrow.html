How it works

Greenery as a service, in three parts.

A fresh rotation of local and exotic plant life — without the hassle of sourcing, pricing or plant-parenting.

1 · Tailor & subscribe

Build your plant package right here — the amount and style of floral display that suits your home or business — and subscribe with one simple monthly payment.

2 · We deliver & place

Once your package is finalised, one of our team delivers everything to your door — potted, placed and arranged to suit the space.

3 · We care & rotate

We keep every plant thriving — watering, feeding, wiping leaves — and rotate a fresh selection into your space regularly, so it never stands still.

Questions

The honest answers.

What if I kill a plant between visits?

Then we've chosen the wrong plant for you — that's on us. We'll swap it free on the next visit and pick something hardier. There's no penalty, no charge, and genuinely no judgement.

Do I need to do anything at all?

Ideally nothing. On the home plans we set everything up so plants comfortably last the month between visits. If a plant would appreciate a splash of water in a heatwave, we'll text you — that's the extent of it.

Do I own the plants and pots?

They're ours — that's what keeps swaps free and your space always looking fresh. Fall in love with a particular plant? You can buy it out anytime and we'll pot its replacement.

Which areas do you cover?

Glasgow and immediate surrounds. If you're a bit further out, get in touch anyway — if you're near an existing round, we can usually make it work.

Can I upgrade or get a fresh rotation early?

Yes to both. Add more plants or step up to a higher-appeal selection anytime — your subscription simply adjusts. Fancy a refresh before your scheduled rotation? A small early-rotation charge brings your next selection round ahead of time.

Can I cancel?

Anytime — plans are monthly rolling. We'll collect the plants and pots on a final visit and part as friends.
